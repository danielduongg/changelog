How it works

Three steps. Then time does the rest.

The whole point is low effort now, high payoff later.

1

Snapshot

Spend two minutes capturing where life is right now — work, city, who you're close to, energy, money, goals, a one-line headline.

2

Live your life

Come back every month or few. Each snapshot is a commit — a saved point you can always return to.

3

Diff any two moments

Pick a “from” and a “to” and watch the changelog write itself: what changed, by how much, what you finished, what you let go.
